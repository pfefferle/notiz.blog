<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>MyOpenID for Domains</em> bietet die Möglichkeit, den <a href="http://www.myopenid.com">MyOpenID Service</a> mit einer <a href="https://www.myopenid.com/product_domains">eigenen Domain</a> zu nutzen.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:quote -->
<blockquote class="wp-block-quote">
	<p>myOpenID for Domains is a new service that helps you give members of your small business, family, or organization an OpenID URL at your own domain. Members get all the features of myOpenID, but get a URL at your domain instead of at myopenid.com.</p>
</blockquote>
